My local matters.

My name is Kerren. And these are the places (and people) I love. I started this site to raise awareness on how we can protect New Zealand's conservation lands.

ALERT! Right now the government wants to change the why and the how of the Conservation Act. At present we, as the public, through conservation boards get a say in how our public lands are used. The proposed changes not only open public lands to ‘disposal’ (for commercial use) but also places that sometimes irreversible decision in the hands of one person — a politician. Our forests, mountains and rivers deserve a more thoughtful approach.

Some things aren't for sale.
